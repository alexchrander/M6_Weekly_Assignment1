📝 Poem (English)

As morning breaks on Denmark's shore,
Three cities await, with weather in store.
Aalborg's gentle breeze whispers low,
With scarce precipitation, the sun may glow.
Odense, however, weeps with rainy tears,
And winds that howl, dispelling all cheers.
Randers, serene, with barely a drop,
And gentle winds, that softly topple and stop.
If I were to choose, where to roam,
I'd pick Randers, and bask in its gentle home.

Og blide vinde, der sagte vipper og standsner.

Ville jeg vælge Randers, og sole mig i dets blide hjem.
