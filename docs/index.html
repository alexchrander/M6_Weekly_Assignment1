📝 Poem (English)

As morning dawns on Denmark's shore,
Three cities await, with weather in store.
Aalborg's breeze is gentle, soft and low,
While Odense's winds do howl and blow.
Randers stands tall, with warmth in its air,

Er Aalborg stedet, kom hvad der kommer må.
